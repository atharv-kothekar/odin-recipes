Blueberry Tarts

"Sam loved to listen to music and make his own songs, to wear soft velvets, to play in the castle kitchen beside the cooks, drinking in the rich smells as he snitched lemon cakes and blueberry tarts."

—A GAME OF THRONES

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 Cup Milk
  • 3/4 Cup Heavy Cream
  • 1/4 Cup Wine
  • Saffron
  • Ginger
  • 3 Egg Yolks
  • 1 Whole Egg
  • 1/2 Cup Honey
  • 1 Pint Blueberries
  • 1 Batch Pre-baked Pastry Dough

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 375° F.
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the milk, cream, wine, saffron and ginger.
  3. Bring the mixture to a boil, then kill the heat.
  4. In a separate bowl, beat the egg yolks, whole egg and honey together.
  5. While beating, add 1/4th of the hot milk mixture.
  6. Pour the egg mixture back into the pot, all the while whisking vigorously to avoid curdling.
  7. Evenly spread the blueberries onto the cooled pastry dough shells.
  8. Spoon the cream mixture over the fruit.
  9. Bake for ~45 minutes.
  10. Allow the tarts to cool before slicing and serving.
